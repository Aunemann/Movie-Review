The Boy Who Harnessed The Wind

William Kamkwamba is the main character of the movie.

William Kamkwamba is also a real person because the film is based off his story and how he managed to create electricity.

The plot is about a young boy who cant afford school because of the dry season for the crops his family produce. In return there becomes a shortage of food in the village and people begin stealing from eachother. He wants to make electricity for the village so they can have running water through the crops.

The setting is located in a village in Kasung, Malawi. It is really dry and people struggle to live there.

The movie's Genre is Family/Drama and I think that fits the movie pretty well.
